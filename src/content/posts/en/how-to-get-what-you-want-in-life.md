In the title of this video I did a bit of clickbait, so I'm going to give you the quick and simple answer. How do you get what you want in life? Easy: you grab a blender, throw in a banana, a scoop of whey protein, some grapes, unicorn blood and a mandrake leaf. You blend it, drink it, and that's it. Done.

No. How do you get what you want in life? Get off your ass and go get it. That's it. No more mystery. And this is coming from someone who finished a physics degree in four years without failing a single exam and trained two hours every day. Who had a free year and got his driver's license, started a company, and was teaching classes.

Don't waste your time. Use it. It's all you have. Invest it in learning. Not just courses and degrees — read, think. Invest your time in useful things that move you closer to who you want to be or what you want to achieve.

No video games, no TV shows, no garbage that eats your time. Don't do it. You know it's screwing you over. And today it's very hard not to, because we live in a pleasure society. Huxley would be incredibly proud. Do you know how much time a TV show takes from you? If it has five seasons of, I don't know, ten episodes at an hour each, that's 50 hours. Do you know what you can do with 50 hours? Imagine someone's paying you 12 euros an hour. Do you know how much money and experience you can get out of that?

So don't come at me with "I don't have time." And I'm not telling you to stop watching movies or whatever. I'm telling you to be consistent — if you really want something, put the hours in. Put in the hours. If you want to watch a movie on a Friday night, go ahead. If before bed you feel like reading something that makes you think, something that's actually enjoyable, sure, watch a movie. Hang out with your friends. But individual, isolated activities that give you absolutely nothing in return, that produce nothing worthwhile — cut them out. Cut them out because they add up.

And if over the years you spend each day investing your time in yourself, your productivity and who you are grows enormously. After 10 accumulated years of that, you're going to be a machine. Because unlike 90% of people, you won't have wasted your time. So get your act together, stop making excuses, stop throwing your time in the trash, focus on your goals and go after them.
