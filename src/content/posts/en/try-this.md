I want you to try something. The next time you watch a video, or put on music, do it with full attention. Without doing anything else. Without your phone in your hand, without looking at other tabs. Just you and the video, or you and the music.

It sounds obvious, but the thing is we no longer know how to do that. We always have to be doing two things at once. And what happens is we enjoy neither of them.

Try it. Really. It's a completely different experience.
